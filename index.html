Map Members

Retrieve members of a ward who live in a defined geographical region. You must be logged into LDS.org for the bookmarklet to function.

Note: The Map Members bookmarklet loads three scripts from 3rd party sources, specifically: the current site, cdnjs.org, and Google Maps.
